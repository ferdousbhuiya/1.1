"Allow me to introduce myself: I am Md Ferdouse Hossain Bhuiya, a proficient Data Analyst and accomplished Electrical Engineer hailing from the United States of America."

I possess over two decades of experience as an Electrical Engineer. My most recent role was with the Al Ain Distribution Company in the United Arab Emirates, where I worked as part of a team responsible for the distribution of water and electricity in both urban and rural areas of Al Ain city. During this time, I accumulated a decade of expertise in the installation and maintenance of 11KV and 33KV Electrical Overhead lines. Additionally, I honed my skills in maintaining automatic tripping devices such as Auto Reclosers, Sectionalizers, and Earth-fault Indicators.

Moreover, i am pursuing my fully research-based Masters in Electrical Engineering at UTHM, Malaysia. Where i am using MATLAB for simulation. By this research I will find a solution for High Impedance fault in MV 11 KV Overhead Line.

Professionally, I am skilled in Data Analysis, Data Extraction, Python, SQL Development, and MSBI for proficiently handling data through Extraction, Transformation, and Loading, complemented by visualization using Power BI and Tableau. In addition, I possess extensive training and practical experience in MAXIMO, arc GIS, and various aspects of Electrical safety within my field of expertise..
